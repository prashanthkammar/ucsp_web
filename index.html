Blog

UCSP is a unique and innovative initiative from the department of ECE, NIE to bring research to UG students in a revolutionary way. Aiming at achieving this, here we introduce the blog. The main focus of this will be exploring new domains, encouraging technical writing as well as information exchange. This platform can be used if you need clarifications regarding any technical concept, if you want to share any new idea or if you have any suggestions/doubts related to our events or projects. We hope you make the best of it. Happy reading!
